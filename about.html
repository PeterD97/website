This website is the perfect open door to learning things about the marvel universe that you may not have known before. we look deep into the characters within the comic books who they are, their belief's and their background/origin stories. We also look into how many different comic books and characters there are and how different all characters come together.

If this is your first glimpse into the Marvel universe then you are in for a roller coaster. This website is going to introduce you to the Magnificent world that was created by Stan Lee and all the unique stories told within his comic books. While we don't wish to spoil anything significant for our newbies who are only seeing this for the first time for all who are already familiar the Marvel universe this is your chance to learn much that you never knew before.

This website will discuss many different characters and explain who they are, where they began both in Marvel comics and their origins, which comic books they've been in and whether they're heroes or villains. The feuds and rivalries between heroes and villians will be discussed on this website too and the different organisations that operate in the stories told in the Marvel universe.
